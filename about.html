Types of coffee

It's no wonder that coffee drinks can be confusing to keep track of with so many options, it seems like the list is endless. To make things a little simpler for you, we've compiled 15 of the most popular types of coffee drinks, which are all simply different combinations of coffee beans, milk, and sugar. So whether you're ordering at your favourite cafe or looking to whip up something new at home, this guide will give you a great starting point.
